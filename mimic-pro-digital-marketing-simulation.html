Mimic Pro Digital Marketing Simulation
Being enrolled in my university’s digital marketing course, I worked with my team to apply the concepts that we were learning in class to a semester-long simulation game. Every student in class managed tens of thousands of simulated ad dollars, practicing A/B testing and conversion rate optimization, conducting key word research, building strategies, and more. In the simulation, students act as an online marketing manager for Kent’s Camera Castle store. Kent's Camera Castle is a camera retailer that has dominated the local market for cameras but has a struggling online presence. They need to revitalize their digital marketing strategy to increase sales for their online business.
Project Date
2020
The Challenge
Plan, create and execute a full digital campaign for Kent's Camera Castle to optimize conversion, sales, and profit. Utilize a $50,000 budget over 8 rounds for PPC advertising, landing page optimization and SEO, and email marketing.

Our Approach
  • Target Market: Beginner & professional photographers → advertise and creating landing pages on beginner-friendly & professional cameras
  • Use bottom of the funnel content and clear CTA while highlighting Kent’s Camera Castle’s value proposition
  • Include long-tail keywords to boost PPC search traffic
  • Use each round ad an opportunity to evaluate what’s performing well and need improvements, and make adjusted changes
Iterate
  • Optimize keyword list to ensure quality and relevancy
  • Ensured ad content is consistent with the corresponding landing page content
  • Ensured unique selling propositions and CTA match up between the email and the landing page
  • Conducted A/B testing to determine winning copy and optimize conversions
